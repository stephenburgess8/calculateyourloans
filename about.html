Calculate Your Loans

Calculate Your Loans is an amortization calculator designed and development to help people understand how much having a loan will cost and the ideal salary range for different amounts of debt.

Please email me at stephenburgess@protonmail.com if you have any feedback or questions about this page.

Overview

Student loan debt represents 6% of the national debt in the United States, and yet many people don’t understand how how much taking out a loan will cost. Since financial literacy is underemphasized in public education, I felt this topic was one from which many people, not just students, could benefit.
