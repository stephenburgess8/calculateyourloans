Final Project

Creating User Interfaces (MAT3440)
Purchase College, State University of New York
Spring 2014
An educational website concerning learning about loans

Overview

Student loan debt represents 6% of the national debt in the United States, and yet many people don’t understand how loans work in a mathematical sense. Since finance is notably under-emphasized in secondary education, I felt this topic was one from which many people, not just students, could benefit. I introduce the web browsers to basic concepts of compound interest, and eventually lead them to a calculator used to find out specifics of how interest collects on loans, and what kind of salary is necessary to pay back loans within the borrower’s time frame. I use short quiz questions to help users learn information.
